# OpsConduit Launch Offer

## Positioning

OpsConduit builds customer-hosted MCP connectors for vertical business tools.

The first product is a read-only Jobber operations assistant. It lets a business owner or manager ask operational questions from an MCP client without changing Jobber data.

## Launch Pricing

Use launch pricing until there are at least three paid customers or one strong repeatable channel.

| Offer | Price | Purpose |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Async Install Help | $149 one-time | Help a technical customer install and configure the connector themselves. |
| Pilot Setup | $399 one-time | Configure the connector with the customer, validate read-only access, and provide a short usage guide. |
| Maintenance | $79/month | Dependency updates, setup troubleshooting, and compatibility checks while the connector is in active use. |
| Custom Connector Work | Quote only | Anything beyond the standard Jobber read-only connector. |

Do not lead cold outreach with every price. Lead with the problem and a demo/install-notes offer. Mention launch pricing only after interest or on the repo/landing material.
