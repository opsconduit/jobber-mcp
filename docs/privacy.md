# OpsConduit Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-15

OpsConduit is an early-stage connector and support service for read-only operational reporting.

## Information We Process

Depending on how a customer uses OpsConduit, the software may process operational records from connected systems such as clients, requests, quotes, jobs, scheduled items, invoices, and user records. The exact data depends on the scopes the customer authorizes.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide connector functionality, generate operational summaries, troubleshoot installations, and support customer-requested workflows.

## Storage

OpsConduit is intended to be customer-hosted by default. We do not require customers to send their operational data to an OpsConduit-hosted service for the initial connector. If support requires reviewing logs or configuration, customers should remove secrets and sensitive data before sending them.

## Third Parties

Customers may connect OpsConduit to third-party services such as Jobber, AI assistants, hosting providers, or payment processors. Those services are governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

## Secrets

Customers should not email API keys, OAuth client secrets, refresh tokens, or private credentials. Secrets should be stored in the customer's local environment or secret manager.

## Retention

Customer-hosted data retention is controlled by the customer. Support emails and business records may be retained as needed for normal operations, accounting, and legal compliance.
